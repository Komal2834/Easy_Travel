ABOUT US

The bamboo which is not even burnt in the pyre is the bamboo we burns every day in the house. Can't believe it? Yes we burn it everyday In the form of incense sticks. In our scriptures it is said that Pitru dosha is created by burning bamboo. Bamboo contains lead and other heavy metals, and if bamboo is burned, these heavy metals and lead oxidize to form lead oxides and heavy metal oxides. Bamboo wood is also considered auspicious in architecture, astrology and religion. A bamboo plant is kept in the house for happiness, prosperity and progress and incense sticks are made from the same bamboo. Burning is considered a sign of inauspicious arrival.
